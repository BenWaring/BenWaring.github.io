Mafia Turn based game Concept

This game is based off of similar games like XCOM and Massive Chalice. The player takes on the role of a mob boss in the 1920's in a fictional world. The players expand their enterprise and harrass business owners to grow their own pockets. Combat would be left to missions where the gameplay is turned based.
