During my second year of studies, I worked in a group project. We had four months to come up with an idea and create a prototype for a world war two bomber game.

Specifically I worked on the design and look of the game. I created the model for the main bomber and the game environment. The game is in a very basic stage however, we were invited to the university showcase where the group won "Best Military Exhibit" out of the entire showcase.
